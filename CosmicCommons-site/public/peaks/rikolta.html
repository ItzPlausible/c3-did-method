Investment Thesis

American agriculture stands at an inflection point. Farm consolidation has concentrated land ownership while extractive technology platforms capture the value of farmer-generated data. Meanwhile, soil degradation threatens long-term productivity and climate volatility increases production risk.

Rikolta inverts this trajectory by funding technology designed for cooperative ownership. With over 2,000 agricultural cooperatives already operating across America, the infrastructure for farmer-owned technology already exists‚Äîit simply needs the right tools.

E2C Pathway

Rikolta portfolio companies develop technology serving existing agricultural cooperatives.
