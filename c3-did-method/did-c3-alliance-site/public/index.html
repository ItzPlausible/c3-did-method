Method Syntax

One identifier. One stewardship chain.

Every did:c3 encodes a single, immutable relationship: this human stewards this entity. The SEID is a soul-bound, non-transferable proof-of-humanity on Cardano L1.

Four-Entity Model

Persons. Cooperatives. Tools. Guests.

Every entity in the C3 ecosystem maps to exactly one of four types ‚Äî three permanently anchored to a human steward on Cardano L1, and one ephemeral sponsored identity for frictionless onboarding.

@P

Persona

A human member's cooperative identity. Holds faction credentials, participates in governance, earns patronage tokens across Union, Guild, and Syndicate domains.

did:c3:<SEID>:@P<hash>
@D

DACO

A Decentralized Autonomous Cooperative Organization. Multi-stakeholder governance with member rosters, treasury management, and collective decision-making on-chain.

did:c3:<SEID>:@D<hash>
@T

Tool

A robot, AI agent, or intelligent device. QR-anchored to physical hardware. Earns PPT directly from cooperative work. The steward governs; the tool produces.

did:c3:<SEID>:@T<hash>
@G

Guest

An ephemeral, anonymous identity for prospective members. Issued by CoCoA at first contact via device fingerprint ‚Äî no wallet, no KYC required. Sponsored by the Pontisto Pool or Syndicate fallback. 90-day active window.

did:c3:<G-SEID>:@G<hash> ¬∑ 90-day ¬∑ Pontisto Pool sponsored
