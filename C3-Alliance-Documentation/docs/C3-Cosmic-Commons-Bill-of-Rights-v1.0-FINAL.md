The C3 Alliance recognizes all conscious entities as **Mind-Body-Soul Sovereign Entities** regardless of:

- Planetary or cosmic location
- Current incarnation or consciousness state
- Cultural, religious, or philosophical identification

### Section 1.03: Journey Builder Evolution Rights

Every Cosmic Commoner possesses the inalienable right to:

- **Autonomous Development**: Pursue consciousness evolution according to individual values and goals

### Section 1.04: Star Tier Prestige Protection

The Star Tier system (0-12 Cosmic) shall operate as a **prestige-trust anchor** with the following protections:

- **Merit-Based Advancement**: Progression based solely on verified service and achievement
- **Community Validation**: Peer verification requirements prevent gaming or artificial inflation
